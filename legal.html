Privacy

Jancadoes is a demo photo-enhancement service. We keep what we collect to the minimum we need to run the product.

What we collect

  • Account data. Your email, a name you choose, and your password (stored as a scrypt hash with a per-account salt — never in plain text).
  • Uploaded photos. The image you send to /api/enhance. It is held in memory only for the duration of the request, sent to OpenAI's image-edit API, and discarded as soon as the response is returned. We do not write it to disk on our server.
  • A session token. A signed JWT we issue at login. Stored in your browser's localStorage; the token jti goes in our database only if you explicitly log out, so that token can't be reused.

What we do with it

  • We do not sell or share your data.
  • We do not use your photos to train any model.
  • Photos pass through OpenAI's API; once there they are subject to OpenAI's policies.

Your data, your call

You can ask us to delete your account at any time by contacting us via the project repository.
